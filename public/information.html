Information

This website is the result of a social impact work created by students of the Complutense University of Madrid, corresponding to the degree of Software Engineering of the Faculty of Computer Science. The aim is to make users who visit the website aware of the relevance of Fake News in today's society. In order to do this, users can play a game with the target of advising which of the news that appear are fake or true.

In addition we include a manual in English version that can also be downloaded in Spanish version to combat and stop the Fake News. As a complement to the page and the class work, a Twitter bot (@URLBOT) has been created to tweet fake news.
